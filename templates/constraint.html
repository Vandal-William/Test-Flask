Groupe: 3 Lister les instruments de chaque groupe

SELECT g.name, GROUP_CONCAT(DISTINCT a.instrument ORDER BY a.instrument SEPARATOR ', ') AS instruments FROM `group` g LEFT JOIN artist a ON g.id = a.id GROUP BY g.id

Groupe: 3 Lister les salles/lieux et les contraintes techniques de chacune.

SELECT p.name, tc.equipment, tc.description FROM place p LEFT JOIN technical_constraints_place tcp ON p.id = tcp.place_id LEFT JOIN technical_constraints tc ON tcp.technical_constraints_id = tc.id;
